About

I’m a graphic designer based in Sacramento with experience across branding, print design, marketing materials, and event graphics. I focus on building visuals that feel purposeful, memorable, and easy to understand.

My process is simple:

  • Understand the message
  • Build a strong visual direction
  • Deliver clean, professional design

I work with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and digital illustration tools to bring ideas to life.
